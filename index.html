Our Story

Fifty Years of
Fragrant Heritage

Founded in 1974, Swiss Arabian Perfumes Group has been crafting extraordinary fragrances that bridge the ancient world of Arabian oud and the precision of Swiss artisanship. From our home in Dubai, we bring you scents that tell the story of a timeless culture.

Our master perfumers travel across the globe to source the rarest ingredients — oud from Assam, rose from Taif, sandalwood from Mysore — blending them into compositions that endure for decades.
